GENERAL RULES
The basics. Read 'em. Know 'em. Live 'em.

Welcome to Last Call RP

Last Call RP is a whitelisted, 18+ roleplay community built for people who want quality RP without all the drama. We're here to have fun, tell great stories, and build a city worth living in. These rules keep things fair for everyone at the bar.

1. The Golden Rule

  • Do not DM STAFF OR OWNER, FOR ANY REASON.
  • Respect everyone — players, staff, streamers, newcomers. We're all here to have fun. Treat people how you want to be treated, or find a different server.
  • Don't be a rule lawyer. If you're looking for a loophole, you're doing it wrong.
  • Use common sense. If something feels like it would ruin someone else's experience, it probably will.

2. Age & Identity

  • You must be 18+ to play. No exceptions, no workarounds.
  • One character account per person. Don't share accounts.
  • Your forum name must match or clearly link to your in-game name.
  • You should have a well thought out backstory for your character.  Using A.I. to write your backstory will result in a denial of your application.
  • Please keep in mind that you play with other people.  It is the staff's discretion to ask you to change your character's name before approval.

3. Communication Standards

  • Keep OOC (out-of-character) chat clean and minimal in-game. Use Discord for OOC discussions.
  • No racist, sexist, homophobic, or otherwise hateful language — in-character OR out. There's no 'my character is racist' exception.
  • No doxxing, real-world threats, or harassment of any kind. Ever.
  • If you have a problem with a player, take it to staff — don't take it into the RP.

4. Whitelisting & Whitelisted Jobs

  • Some jobs (police, EMS, DOJ, etc.) require a whitelist application and approval. Don't show up in a cop uniform without one.
  • Whitelisted faction members are expected to be active and maintain conduct standards. Inactivity without notice may result in removal.
  • Going off-duty doesn't mean going off-standards. You represent the faction even in civilian clothes.

5. Streaming & Recording

  • You're welcome to stream Last Call RP. Don't use your stream to gain IC advantages (stream sniping = ban).
  • Don't break scenes to wave at your camera. Keep it immersive.
  • If someone asks not to appear on stream, respect it and keep them off-cam.

6. Bans & Punishments

  • Punishments range from verbal warnings to permanent bans depending on severity.
  • Ban evasion with alternate accounts results in a permanent ban on all accounts.
  • You can appeal any ban through our Discord ticket system. Be respectful in appeals — hostility gets your appeal closed.
  • Staff decisions are final. If you disagree, use the proper appeal channels, not public drama.

Last revised by Last Call RP Staff Team. Check #announcements for updates.
