What We Expect from Every Player

Good RP isn't just about knowing the rules — it's about adding to the world, not subtracting from it. Every interaction should leave things more interesting than you found them.

1. Fear RP

  • Your character values their life. When there's a gun in your face, act like it.
  • You cannot ignore threats to your life just because you think you could 'win' the situation OOC.
  • Running from someone actively pointing a firearm at you (within 10 feet) is a Fear RP violation.
  • Vehicles don't make you immortal. Getting rammed or shot at should still cause genuine fear responses. Flooring it out of there is a valid fear response!

2. New Life Rule (NLR)

  • After your character is downed and revived by EMS (or respawns), they have fuzzy memories of the events leading to their death.
  • You cannot return to active combat zones for 15 minutes after being revived.
  • You cannot use OOC knowledge from before your death to drive IC decisions.
  • NLR does NOT mean your character gets a full personality wipe — just memory of that specific incident.

3. Powergaming

Powergaming = forcing unrealistic outcomes or using mechanics in ways that deny others meaningful roleplay.

  • Don't use /me to make other characters do things (e.g., '/me grabs John's gun' — you don't get to decide what happens to John's gun).
  • Don't exploit game mechanics to achieve things your character realistically couldn't do.
  • Don’t abuse in-game glitches, hack software, or anything that could be interpreted as such to gain an advantage.  
  • Winning isn't the goal. Story is the goal.
  • Do not loiter in green zones to avoid conflict.

4. Metagaming

  • Information from Discord, Twitch streams, or OOC chats cannot be used in-character.
  • Radio channels are IC. Don't create private Discord calls to relay IC information.
  • This includes weekly meetings for organizations.  Churches should be held IC only and should NOT be held in discord!
  • You cannot recognize someone just because you know their Discord/Twitch name.
  • Telling someone in Discord 'help I'm being robbed' while it's happening IC is metagaming.
  • Do NOT voice ID characters IC. If the person is “blacked out” it means they are unknown. You can not know this person IC.
  • You are allowed multiple characters, they are not allowed to share information, and you as a player need to keep them separate. They cannot know each other.

5. Random Deathmatch (RDM) & Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM)

  • RDM: Don't attack or kill someone without a valid RP reason. Boredom is not a valid reason.
  • VDM: Don't use your vehicle as a weapon.
  • There must be prior RP engagement before violence. Drive-bys on strangers with zero prior interaction = instant punishment.
  • Birdy beef and other non direct forms of communication do not count as proper engagement. Prior engagement is required to be direct person to person.

6. Combat

  • Don't disconnect to avoid being robbed, arrested, killed, or facing any other IC consequence.
  • If your game crashes mid-scene, return ASAP and notify the other parties. Utilize the headpops channels.
  • In Police vs Criminal combat: Police are allowed up to double the player count of criminals.
  • All other combat encounters are to be limited to 4 v 4
  • Whitelisted gangs will have the ability to have more combatants. See gang rules
  • The illegal dealers missions are “red zones”, In red zones, shooting on sight is permitted, no initiation required, up 5 on 5.
  • Hospital grounds are a “green zone” meaning combat is prohibited. In businesses combat is heavily discouraged, employees are not to be targets of any crime that is committed.
  • EMS are not valid targets for criminal activities. The only exception can be with combat medics, who show up during a shoot out.
  • Do not bring combat encounters into greenzones or businesses.

7. RP Continuity & Scene Endings

  • Don't break character in the middle of active scenes without a genuine emergency.
  • If you need to pause a scene, try your absolute best to RP a reason for it, ie your character needs to use the bathroom, take a phone call. Make use of emotes if you have to step away!
  • Try to move your character out of the scene when stepping out, to avoid confusion.

8. Sensitive RP Topics

Last Call RP allows darker RP themes as part of storytelling — but with guardrails.

  • ERP is not allowed in the city, we do not want 50 shades of gray in the server logs.
  • Sexual RP involving minors is an immediate permanent ban. NO EXCEPTIONS!!!
  • Torture, graphic violence, or trauma RP requires a check-in with all participants.
  • Absolutely no scenarios involving children. This is an immediate permanent ban.  NO EXCEPTIONS!

Great RP makes everyone look good — including your 'villain.' Play to the story, not just the win.
