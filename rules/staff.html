STAFF POLICIES
Who they are, what they do, and how to deal with them.

Meet the Staff Team

Staff at Last Call RP are volunteers who keep the lights on and the peace between scenes. They're players too — they have characters, crews, and stories. The difference is they're also responsible for making sure everyone gets a fair shot at good RP. Treat them accordingly.

1. Staff Roles

  • Owner: Final say on all policy, direction, and appeals. Rarely in admin sits — they're the last line.
  • Senior Admin: Oversees staffing, major ban reviews, and policy updates.
  • Admin: Handles ban enforcement, appeals, and escalated player issues.
  • Moderator: Front-line staff. Handles in-game admin sits, reports, and minor infractions.
  • Support: Helps new players, answers questions, manages tickets. Not enforcement-focused.

2. Admin Sits

  • An admin sit is a moderated out-of-character meeting to resolve a dispute or report. All parties must be respectful — yes, even if you think you're right.
  • Don't call admin sits to win an argument. Call them for genuine rule violations.
  • Lying in an admin sit is itself a punishable offense. We check logs.
  • You may be asked to provide video evidence. Keep recordings if you're in a contested situation.
  • Admin sits that happen mid-scene will be resolved ASAP — staff try to get you back in play quickly.

3. Reporting a Player

  • Use the #player-report channel or in-game /report to file reports.
  • Include: your name, their name, what happened, when it happened, and evidence if available.
  • Don't report the same person multiple times for the same incident. One report, let staff handle it.
  • Retaliatory reports (filing a report because someone reported you first) are taken very seriously.

4. Appealing Decisions

  • Bans, warnings, and faction removals can all be appealed through the Discord ticket system.
  • Appeals must be submitted within 7 days of the action taken.
  • Be specific and calm in your appeal. 'This is unfair' is not an argument. Explain what happened from your perspective.
  • A Senior Admin or Owner reviews all appeals. Their decision is final.
  • You cannot appeal a decision while actively causing problems in the community — clean up first.

5. Staff Conduct Standards

  • Staff are held to the same rules as everyone else — plus a bit more.
  • Staff cannot abuse their position for IC advantages (spawning items, teleporting unfairly, looking at player locations).
  • Staff must recuse themselves from situations involving their own characters, friends, or faction members.
  • Corruption reports against staff go straight to Senior Admin or Owner — use the #staff-report channel.
  • Staff are not immune from bans. Abuse of power is taken seriously.

6. Contacting Staff

  • For urgent in-game issues: use /report in game.
  • For general questions and ticket-based issues: Discord is your go-to.
  • Don't DM staff individually about reports or bans unless they've asked you to. Use the proper channels so it's tracked.
  • Don't ping staff repeatedly. They'll get to you. Spamming slows everything down.

7. Staff Availability

Staff are volunteers. Response times vary. Peak hours see faster responses — off-peak may take longer. If something is urgent and no staff are available, document it (screenshot/video) and file a report as soon as someone's on.

Staff aren't here to police the fun — they're here to protect it. Work with them, not against them.
